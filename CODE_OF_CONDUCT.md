# Code of Conduct

Meeting Note is a practical open-source project for meeting recording and notes.
Keep project communication useful, respectful, and focused on improving the app.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, specific, and respectful.
- Assume people have different operating systems, hardware, languages, and levels
  of audio/debugging experience.
- Keep bug reports and discussions free of private meeting content, API keys,
  personal data, and confidential logs.
- Disagree with ideas and implementation choices, not with people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Posting secrets, meeting transcripts, audio, or logs that you do not have the
  right to share.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or repeated low-effort comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and close issues or discussions
that do not follow these rules. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a ban
from the project.
