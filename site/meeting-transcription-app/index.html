From recording to transcript

Meeting Note records meeting audio and sends it to OpenAI speech-to-text models using your API key. The transcript is saved into local history for search and review.

Search what was said

Instead of replaying a long call, search for a customer name, decision, deadline, or action item inside the transcript.

Export when the transcript is ready

Export notes to TXT, Markdown, or DOCX so meeting outcomes can move into your existing workflow.

Use transcription first, then generate summaries and action items.
