Record meeting audio from your PC

Meeting Note uses Windows WASAPI loopback to capture system audio. You can also mix microphone audio when you need your own voice in the recording.

Built for recurring calls

The app keeps a searchable local history, so recordings, transcripts, and summaries do not disappear after the call ends.

Best fit

  • Windows users who want meeting notes without a browser SaaS tool.
  • Founders, consultants, and operators who need searchable call records.
  • Open-source users who want to inspect the project before using it.
